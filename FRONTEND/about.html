What is K2.OS?

K2.OS (K2 Operating System) is an AI-powered personal operating system designed to help students manage tasks, goals, projects, ideas, journaling, and personal growth through one intelligent command center.

Project Vision

Our vision is to build an intelligent productivity platform that combines AI, planning, reflection, and personal growth into one simple and powerful system for students.

Main Features

  • Task Management
  • Goal Tracking
  • Project Organization
  • AI Assistant
  • Daily Journal
  • Analytics Dashboard
  • Productivity Tracking
  • Personal Growth
